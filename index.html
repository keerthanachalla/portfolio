About

Hello there! I'm Keerthana, a Frontend / Fullstack-Jamstack web developer based in Bengaluru. I wake up in the morning, play a football game, have a sumptuous breakfast, open up my laptop and build things that live on the web all day, everyday.

People who know me say I'm obsessed, that's fair, I am very driven and love learning new things.

Besides being a web developer, I am a football organizer from my city that focuses on making football more accesible to the women. I am also a gardener who grows and tends to all the mostly edible greens growing in my terrace garden. I don't take this life for granted and fearlessly choose to do the things that free me, educate me and make me what I want to become. All that, plus, I am a cat person.
